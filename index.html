Our Aims and Objectives

The objectives of the union shall be:

1. To maintain human rights.
2. To promote the social, cultural, intellectual and recreational interests of its members.
3. To foster and protect the interest of its members in pursuance of its aims and objectives.
4. To promote the good name of LAUTECH and to cherish, uphold and further the highest traditions of the institution.
5. To maintain contacts and harmonize with those of other unions whose aims and objectives are acceptable to the union.
6. To establish effective group communication with the university officials so that the rights, privileges and obligation of union members are maintained and protected.
